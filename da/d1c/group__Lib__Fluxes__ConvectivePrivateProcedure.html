Function for computing left eigenvectors matrix (L) of the Jacobian fluxes matrix in primitive variables form.

Note:
This function consider only the normal direction: where P are the primitive variables and n is the normal direction. R is the matrix of the right eigenvectors, is the diagonal matrix of the eigenvalues and L is the matrix of the left eigenvectors.
