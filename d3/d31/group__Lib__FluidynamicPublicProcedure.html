Subroutine for imposing the boundary conditions of blocks of grid level "l" updating the ghost cells.

Note:
Considering the ghost cell and the inner one (being the other bound) the available boundary conditions are:
  • REF: reflective boundary condition ;
  • EXT: extrapolation boundary condition ;
  • PER: periodic boundary condition ;
  • ADJ: adjacent (cell) boundary condition where "a" is the adjacent cell (b,i,j,k indexes must be specified);
  • IN1: supersonic inflow steady boundary condition .
