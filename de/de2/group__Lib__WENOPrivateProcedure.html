Subroutine for computing WENO polynomials.

Note
For avoiding the creation of temporary arrays (improving the efficiency) the arrays V and VP are declared as assumed-shape with only the lower bound defined. Their extensions are: V [1:2,1-S:-1+S], VP [1:2,0:S-1].

Function for computing the WENO convolution of the polynomial reconstructions.
