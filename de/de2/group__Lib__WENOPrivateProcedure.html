Subroutine for computing WENO polynomials.

Note:
For avoiding the creation of temporary arrays (improving the efficiency) the arrays V and VP are declared as assumed-shape with only the lower bound defined. Their extentions are: V [1:2,1-S:-1+S], VP [1:2,0:S-1].
Parameters:
[in]SNumber of stencils used.
[in]VVariable to be reconstructed [1:2,1-S:-1+S].
[out]VPPolynomial reconstructions [1:2, 0:S-1 ].
