pure subroutine primitive2conservative::p2c_reshape1D ( integer(I8P), intent(in)  N,
type(Type_Primitive), dimension(1:n), intent(in)  P,
type(Type_Conservative), dimension(1:n), intent(inout)  U 
) [private]

Subroutine for doing the calculation on 1D-reshaped arrays in order to augment computational efficiency.

The computation of the parent procedure is performed in 1D re-shaped arrays in order to augment the computational efficiency when using OpenMP shared parallel paradigm. As a matter of facts the scalability of OpenMP parallelism is exploited only when the parallelized loop is the largest one. In the case of multi-dimensional arrays the first index is not always the largest one. Therefore the 1D reshaping ensures that the OpenMP parallelism is applied on the largest index because after reshaping there is only one index.

Parameters:
[in]NNumber of finite volumes.
[in]PPrimitive variables.
[in,out]UConservative variables.
