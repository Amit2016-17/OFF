Subroutine for imposing extrapolation of ghost cells from internal ones (left boundary).

Note:
For avoiding the creation of temporary arrays (improving the efficiency) the array P is declared as assumed-shape with only the lower bound defined. Its extentions is: P [1-gc:0+gc].
Parameters:
[in]gcNumber of ghost cells.
[in]icNumber of internal cells used for extrapolation (1 or gc).
[in]NNumber of internal cells.
[in,out]PPrimitive variables [1-gc:0+gc].

pure subroutine boundary_conditions::set_adj ( integer(I_P), intent(in)  gc,
type(Type_BC), dimension(1-gc:), intent(in)  bc,
type(Type_Primitive), dimension (1-gc:), intent(inout)  P 
) [private]

Subroutine for imposing adjacent boundary conditions.

Note:
For avoiding the creation of temporary arrays (improving the efficiency) the arrays bc and P are declared as assumed-shape with only the lower bound defined. Their extentions are: bc [1-gc:0], P [1-gc:0].
When this subroutine is called for a 'right' (Ni,Nj,Nk) boundary the section of arrays BC and P must be properly remapped: in the section [1-gc:0] must be passed the actual section [N:N+gc-1] for BC and [N+1:N+gc] for P.
Parameters:
[in]gcNumber of ghost cells.
[in]bcBoundary conditions infos [1-gc:0].
[in,out]PLeft section of primitive variables [1-gc:0].
