subroutine, public lib_runge_kutta::rk_init ( integer(i1p), intent(in)  S)

Subroutine for initializing Runge-Kutta coefficients.

Note
Currently 5 different schemes are available whose coefficients are (see definition in Runge-Kutta library):
  • 1 stage: Forward Euler, one stage, order
  • 2 stages: Strong Stability Preserving, two stages, order
  • 3 stages: Strong Stability Preserving, three stages, order
  • 4 stages: four stages, order
  • 5 stages: Strong Stability Preserving, five stages, order

Parameters
[in]sNumber of stages used.
